About Us

Josh Tech was incorporated in 1998, to focus on the burgeoning Service industry. Josh Tech is head quartered in Bangalore with a service network, spanning the entire country.
Josh Tech has established a specialized card repair facility with a clear focus on Communication Circuit boards.Our forte integrates our technologies both to extend the lifecycles and maximize the ROI of your product through our suite of solutions that include reverse logistics, screening and testing, repair & refurbishing.
Repairing over 3 Million PCBA, a large domain expertise has been built-up for all the Telecom and electronic technologies in India.
